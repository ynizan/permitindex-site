Eligibility

Any individual or entity conducting business in California. Requirements vary based on business structure and industry.

Required Documents

Business name (DBA if applicable)
Business structure documentation
EIN letter from IRS
Physical business address
Owner identification (driver's license or passport)
Industry-specific permits/licenses
