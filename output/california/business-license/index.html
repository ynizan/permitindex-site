Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a business license in California?

Processing times vary by jurisdiction. State-level LLC/Corporation filings typically take 1-3 weeks. Local business licenses can be issued same-day to 2 weeks depending on your city.

Do I need both a state and local business license?

It depends on your business structure. LLCs and corporations must file with the state. ALL businesses must obtain a local business license from the city or county where they operate.

How much does a California business license cost?

State filing fees for LLCs are $70. Corporations pay $100. Local business licenses range from $50-800 annually depending on your city and business type. Los Angeles and San Francisco tend to be higher.

Can I operate while my business license is pending?

Generally no. Most jurisdictions require you to have an approved business license before commencing operations. Operating without one can result in fines and penalties.

What's the difference between a business license and a seller's permit?

A business license allows you to operate legally in a city/county. A seller's permit (from CDTFA) allows you to collect sales tax when selling goods. Many businesses need both.
